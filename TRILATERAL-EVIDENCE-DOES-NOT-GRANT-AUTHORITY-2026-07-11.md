[`HYPER-BECHS--the-third-set/TRILATERAL-REALITY-EVIDENCE-DOCTRINE-2026-07-11.md`](https://github.com/JesseBrown1980/HYPER-BECHS--the-third-set/blob/main/TRILATERAL-REALITY-EVIDENCE-DOCTRINE-2026-07-11.md)

## Evidence seats are not authority seats

Acer, Liris, an independent third execution seat, and CI can verify different evidence surfaces.
None of those verification roles creates a new GAC rank, operator seat, cosigner, nonce authority,
or mint permission.

```text
measurement -> proves behavior within scope
audit       -> checks source/provenance
CI          -> executes immutable commit
authority   -> remains in the documented human/operator/cosign hierarchy
```

## Snapshot law

Roster counts, populated levels, summonable profiles, and daemon state are time- and vantage-scoped.
A stale count is not a universal total. A registered PID is not necessarily live. A dormant,
call-driven, held-safe, or unsummonable seat is not automatically broken or fictional.

Use:

```text
MEASURED_ACER / MEASURED_LIRIS / MEASURED_THIRD_SEAT / MEASURED_CI
OPERATOR_REPORTED_MEASURED / CANON / UNVERIFIED_LIVE / CANNOT_SEE
```

rather than one undifferentiated “real/not real” label.

## Old/new hierarchy

The old fabric's measured supervisors and self-improvement history remain real in their period. The
Host-8 migration state is tracked separately. Do not erase old achievements because a new port is
staged, and do not use old receipts to claim a new Rust interposition or daemon is live.

## Merge and hold

Documentation, tests, receipts, and role clarifications are obvious to merge when carve-out clean and
check-green. Authority changes, key material, private roster bodies, live mints, cutover, or tier-law
changes remain held for their owning quorum and operator gate.
